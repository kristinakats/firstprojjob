COMPANY PROFILE

Agelef Maritime Services Limited acts as shipbrokers representing various ship managers and ship owners. Agelef is responsible to its clients for chartering a substantial fleet of high specification oil tanker and dry cargo vessels in the open market.

The fleet comprises tanker vessels, ranging from VLCC through Suezmax to Aframax and dry cargo vessels ranging from Cape through Kamsarmax to Panamax.

The company’s aim is to provide high quality services to its clients worldwide. Agelef Maritime Services Limited is registered in England and is a member of the Baltic Exchange.
